Purpose of Project

From September 19, 2020 through March 7th, 2021 the Grant Museum of Zoology will launch their Displays of Power exhibition. Each exhibit will address questions such as how these exotic specimens came to live in the heart of London, connecting these specimens to a broader history of science and empire.

For those unable to physically make it to London during this six month limited exhibition,this complementary website will provide virtual interactive displays of some of the specimens and exhibits available. For those who are able to attend the exhibit, the website will serve as a form of interactive advertisement, highlighting some of the displays patrons will have a chance to enjoy in person. Especially important is how this website will encourage interactive engagement with exhibit content, allowing site visitors to interrogate the narratives of power embodied by the specimens featured.
